About

My current research examines language models as media and communicative forms. I am also interested in normative ethics.

Before UC San Diego, I completed an M.A. in Philosophy at CU Boulder with a certificate in Science and Technology Policy Research, and a B.A. in Philosophy at Cal Poly San Luis Obispo. I also consult on technology ethics with Compass Ethics, advising companies developing and deploying novel technologies.

Areas of specialization: ethics of technology, applied ethics, media theory. Areas of competence: bioethics, philosophy of science, continental philosophy and critical theory, philosophy of religion.
